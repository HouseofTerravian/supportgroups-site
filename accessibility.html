Accessibility Statement

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Our Commitment

SupportGroups.online is committed to providing a digital experience that is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We work to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient color contrast across all page elements
  • Keyboard-navigable interface throughout
  • Descriptive alt text on meaningful images
  • Responsive design for all screen sizes and zoom levels
  • No content that flashes more than three times per second

Known Limitations

We are actively working to improve the accessibility of live video sessions and third-party embedded components. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know so we can address it promptly.

Feedback & Contact

We welcome your accessibility feedback. If you experience difficulty accessing any part of this Platform, please contact us:

  • Email: support@supportgroups.online

We aim to respond to accessibility-related inquiries within 2 business days.

Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the relevant accessibility authority in your jurisdiction.
